WHAT IS GRADFLOW?

Built for Students by Students

Gradflow makes it easy to create graduation flowcharts for students. We want to help you visualize your graduation path.
Don't let those prerequisite classes get the best of you! (it did for some of us)
Create your graduation flowchart and hang it up in your room! Show your parents how smart you are for finding the right classes!
Come back to GradFlow every semester and update your flowchart as you go using our easy to use interface!
At the end, you can print to keep it as reference!
