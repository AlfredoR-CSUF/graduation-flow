About

Built for Students by Students

Gradflow makes it easy to create graduation flowcharts for students. We want to help you visualize your graduation path. Don't let those prerequisite classes get the best of you!

Create your graduation flowchart and hang it up in your room! Come back to GradFlow every semester and update your flowchart as you go! Print again, until the end!

Meet the Team

GradFlow is a team effort of CSUF 5 students for our software engineering class.
